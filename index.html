Hi!


I am a Computer Scientist with an MSc in Digital Media - Computational Intelligence. I have 5 years of working experience in Research, Development and Innovation projects funded by EU. I have worked on different phases of H2020 and Horizon Europe projects including writing proposals to attract funds through competitive calls, working on technical project implementation and acting as a Project Manager. During technical project implementation I have worked on multimedia data modeling and analysis using deep neural networks and computer vision and developed applications for object detection and motion based multiple object tracking. I love applying advanced data processing techniques to extract meaningful insights from raw data. I gladly accept new challenges and I am passionate about providing solutions that can make people’s lives better!

The LASIE project will design and develop a novel framework to assist forensic analysts in their investigations.
